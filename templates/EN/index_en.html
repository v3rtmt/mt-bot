MT Strategy

Crypto-Python Bot

About The Bot

This bot is designed to carry out operations in the cryptocurrency futures market, with a defined strategy, the bot automatically carries out operations to all users in the pair they have chosen.

How it Works?

Signals of a strategy are sent through 'POST' methods to different URLs located on a second server, these signals determine when the bot is going to carry out an operation and when it is going to cancel it.

The user will have previously established their API keys given by their exchange and the parameters to operate using the strategy.

Each currency has its strategy and exchanges available, if you can't find your exchange in your favorite currency you can request it here
